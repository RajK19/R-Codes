Assumptions:

1)) Null Hypothesis----
effect of the new medicine launched in the market is equal to the effect of older medicine launched in the market, 
i.e  theoretical value = observed value

2)) Alternative Hypothesis----
(Alternative Hypothesis always contradicts the null hypothesis)
effect of new medicine is always greater than the effect of older medicine launched in the market.

3)) Tests(statistics)

1) χ2 chi square test
2) t - student test
3) Fisher's Z test

4)) Level of Significance (α)
